sorting algorithms: A sorting algorith is used to rearrange a given array oe list element(s) according to a particular pattern( ascending or descending order ).

*** we need sorting for :
1 Efficient sorting is important for optimizing the efficiency of other algorithms (such as search or merge), which required input data in sorted lists.
2 It is easier and faster to locate items in a sorted list.

*** types of sorting :
Selection sort, bubble sort, insertion sort, merge sort, quick sort, heap sort, redix sort etc.
